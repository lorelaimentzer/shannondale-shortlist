Privacy Policy

We believe in being straightforward about what information we collect, how we use it, and what we don't do with it.

Last updated: June 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Shannondale Shortlist ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and handles information when you use our website at shannondaleshorlist.com (the "Site"). We are a small community directory. We do not sell your data, run advertising, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes — ever.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information you voluntarily provide to us. We do not use tracking pixels, behavioral advertising, or data brokers.

What we collect When we collect it Why we collect it
Name, email, phone number When you submit the contractor application or contact form To review your application or respond to your message
Business information, services, credentials When a provider submits an application To vet and create a directory listing
References provided When a provider submits an application To verify community standing where applicable
Message content When you use the contact form To respond to your inquiry
Basic site analytics When you visit the Site To understand how the Site is being used (page visits, not personal data)

We do not collect payment information, social security numbers, government ID numbers, or any sensitive personal data.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information submitted through this Site is used only for the following purposes:

  • Reviewing and responding to contractor applications
  • Creating and maintaining provider listings in the directory
  • Responding to messages sent through the contact form
  • Verifying provider credentials and community standing
  • Communicating with listed providers about their listing status
  • Understanding general Site usage to improve the directory
We do not use your information for advertising, marketing, or to contact you about anything unrelated to your submission. We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with any third party for commercial purposes.

3. Form Submissions and Third-Party Processors

Our contact and application forms are processed through Formspree (formspree.io), a third-party form service. When you submit a form, your information is transmitted to Formspree's servers and delivered to our email inbox. Formspree's own privacy policy governs how they handle form data in transit. You can review their privacy policy at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

We do not use any other third-party data processors, CRM platforms, email marketing services, or advertising networks that receive your personal information.

4. Provider Directory Listings

Information that appears in provider directory listings — including name, business name, service categories, service areas, bio, and badges — is submitted voluntarily by providers for the purpose of being publicly listed. By applying to join the Shannondale Shortlist, providers consent to this information being displayed publicly on the Site.

Provider contact information (phone numbers, email addresses) is displayed on listing profiles at the provider's discretion and with their consent. Homeowners contacting providers through the directory do so directly — Shannondale Shortlist is not a party to those communications.

5. Cookies and Analytics

This Site may use basic, privacy-friendly analytics to understand general traffic patterns — such as how many people visit the Site and which pages are most viewed. This does not involve tracking individual users, storing personal data, or using advertising cookies.

We do not use Google Ads, Facebook Pixel, or any behavioral advertising trackers. We do not use cookies to identify, profile, or retarget individual visitors.

If you use a browser that blocks all cookies, the Site will function normally.

6. Data Retention

We retain form submissions and application data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was submitted:

  • Approved provider applications are retained while the listing is active and for a reasonable period after removal
  • Declined applications are retained briefly for administrative reference and then deleted
  • Contact form messages are retained in our email inbox and deleted periodically

You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us through the Contact page.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of any personal information we hold about you
  • Correction — request that inaccurate information be corrected
  • Deletion — request that your personal information be deleted
  • Withdrawal — withdraw consent for your information to be used (for providers, this means requesting removal from the directory)

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through our Contact page. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

8. Children's Privacy

This Site is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information to this Site, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

9. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect the information submitted through this Site. Form data is transmitted securely via HTTPS. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. We encourage you to use caution when submitting personal information online.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by an updated date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the Site following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

If you have questions, concerns, or requests related to this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please reach out through our Contact page. We read every message personally and will respond promptly.

Shannondale Shortlist is operated by Lorelai & Michael, residents of Shannondale, West Virginia.

Questions or Requests?

Want to access, correct, or delete your information? Have a question about this policy? We're real people and we'll get back to you.
