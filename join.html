Application Received

Thank you for applying to the Shannondale Shortlist. We review every application personally and will be in touch within 5–7 business days.

We appreciate your patience — and for caring enough about this community to want to be part of it.

What to Expect

  • Application reviewed personally within 5–7 business days
  • We may follow up by phone or email for additional information
  • License and insurance verification handled by email after review
  • All applicants receive a personal response — approvals and declines
  • Approved providers receive a warm welcome and onboarding note

We never ghost applicants. You will hear from us.

The Three Listing Tiers

✓ Verified Pro
Licensed trades with insurance documentation on file. For plumbing, electrical, HVAC, and similar.
★ Community Trusted
5+ neighbor vouches required. Great for lawn care, hauling, cleaning. No license required.
⭐ Founder's Pick
By invitation only. Reserved for providers personally used and recommended by Lorelai & Michael.

It's Free to Apply

  • No application fee
  • Basic listing is always free
  • No paid placement — ever
  • Listings based on merit only

Optional enhanced listings may be available in the future, but will never affect your badge status or placement order.
