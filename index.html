How It Works

Built on Trust, Not Algorithms

Every provider on the Shortlist has been personally reviewed. No paid placements. No anonymous reviews. Just real community knowledge, organized.

01
Providers Apply

Contractors and service providers submit an application with their information, credentials, and references.

02
We Review

Every application is personally reviewed. Licenses checked, insurance verified, references contacted where applicable.

03
Community Vouches

Providers without formal credentials can earn a Community Trusted badge with 5 verified neighbor endorsements.

04
They Make the List

Only approved providers are listed. Badges reflect exactly what was verified — nothing more, nothing less.
